Eight Parts of Speech

Words are divided into different kinds
according to the works they do in sentence.
The Parts of Speech are 8 in numbers.

  1. Noun
  2. Verb
  3. Pronoun
  4. Adjective
  5. Adverb
  6. Preposition
  7. Conjunction
  8. Interjection

1.Noun

It is a word used to
name a person,animal,place,thing
or an abstract idea.

Examples:-
dog,cat,garden,work,music,town,teacher,Jack,

The sun shines

Anna goes to school

2.Verb

A verb indicates thought & actions.
They show when the action happened,
how many things were acting &
can describe the action..

Examples:-
run,go,have,invite,laugh,listen,playing,
singing,walk

The girl and boy are dancing.

3.Pronoun

A word which is used in the place of a noun.
It is pro+nun i.e.
it refers to a noun whose identity is made
clear previously.

Examples:-
he,I,its,me,my,she,that,this,those,us,
who,whom,you

Jack is hungry.He wants to eat

4.Adjective

An adjective tells us more about the noun or pronoun i.e.
It gives us more information about
something or someone.

Examples:-
angry,brave,healthy,little,old,smart,small,two,
some,good,big,interesting

Thin cat

Fat cat

5.Adverb

An adeverb tells us more about
the action of the noun(not the noun itself).
It can add to the verb,
adjective or another adverb.

Examples:-
badly,fully,hardly,nearly,never,quickly,
silently,well,very,really,almost

Runs quickly

6.Preposition

A word placed before noun or
a thing referring by it,
has with something else.

Examples:-
above,before,except,from,in,near,of,
since,between,upon,while,with,to,
at,after,on

The butterfly flies near the flower

7.Conjunction

A word that joins
words,sentences and phrases.
It is also known as a joiner as it connects(conjoins)
parts of a sentence.

Examples:-
and,or,but,so,after,before,unless,either,neither,
because,since

I was tired so I went to sleep

8.Interjection

A spoken word that express
an emotion or a sudden feeling of mind.
It ends with the sign of exclamation(!).

Examples:-
aha!,gosh!,great!,hey!,hi!,hooray!,
oh!,ouch!,wel!,phew!,oops!

Oops! I broke the glass
